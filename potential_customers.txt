POTENTIAL CUSTOMERS

1. Businesses of all sizes need email marketing: from small Etsy shops to big brands, almost everyone sends newsletters, promotions, updates, etc.

2. E-commerce stores need beautiful promotional and transactional emails (like order confirmations, shipping updates).

3. Agencies and freelancers often buy templates to speed up client projects.

4. Startups and SaaS companies use email sequences for onboarding and marketing.

5. Event organizers need invitations, reminders, and follow-up emails.

6. Course creators and coaches send out newsletters, promotions, and nurture sequences.
